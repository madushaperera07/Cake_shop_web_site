About PCS

We specialized in creating delicious cakes using fresh ingredients and bringing out their finest to our dessert selection. PCS only use the freshest and finest ingredients in our cakes and pastries, including - real butter, fresh free range eggs, 100% Canadian diary whipped cream, and European chocolate. Each cake and pastry are handcrafted the moment you order. Our handcrafted cakes are delicious works of edible art!

Our Differences:

Simple

Our cakes are freshly baked specifically for each order

Quality

Only the freshest and finest ingredients are used in our cakes and pastries

Local

We support local farms where ever possible

Passion

Our employees from pastry chef to delivery staff are committed in heart and mind
